# Delta Roe — Review Request & Reply Templates

Replace `[REVIEW-LINK]` with the Google review short-link from the GBP dashboard
(see docs/gbp-kit.md, section 8) — or simply use **deltaroe.com/review**, which
works in every template and lets people choose Google or Yelp.

**Policy note (important):** send the ask to *every* client after a session — not
just the happy ones. Filtering who gets asked ("review gating") violates Google's
review policies and can get reviews wiped. One flow for everyone; the /review page
already gives an "email Tamika directly" path for anyone who'd rather talk
privately.

**Timing:** send the same evening or next morning, while the calm is still in
their body. One follow-up a week later if they haven't clicked — then stop.

## Owner replies to reviews

Reply to **every** review within a few days — Google shows response rate, and
future clients read the replies as closely as the reviews. Two rules: never
reveal session details the client didn't mention themselves (their review sets
the boundary of what's public), and never argue.

### 5-star reply — Variant A

> Thank you, [First name] 👑 It was an honor to hold that space for you. What
> you felt in the room, you carry with you — come back anytime your energy asks
> for it. First love yourself. — Tamika, Delta Roe

### 5-star reply — Variant B

> [First name], this made my whole day. Watching someone walk out lighter than
> they walked in is exactly why Delta Roe exists. Thank you for trusting me with
> your energy — the sanctuary is here whenever you need it. 💛 — Tamika

### Critical review reply (3 stars or below)

> [First name], thank you for telling me honestly — I take this seriously.
> Every session at Delta Roe should leave you feeling cared for, and I'm sorry
> this one fell short of that for you. I'd love to make it right: please call me
> directly at (916) 206-1752 or email Info@deltaroe.com so I can hear more and
> invite you back for a session on me. — Tamika, Owner

Notes for critical replies:
- Reply once, warmly, then move the conversation offline. Don't debate details
  publicly, even if the review is unfair — future readers judge the *reply*.
- If a review is fake or violates Google policy (spam, wrong business, hate
  speech), don't reply first — flag it in the GBP dashboard ("Report review")
  and wait for the outcome.
- The comped-session offer is Tamika's call; remove that clause if she'd rather
  not standardize it.
